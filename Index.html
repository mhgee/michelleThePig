Welcome

Welcome to my food blog, Michelle the Pig! This is where I post all of my food reviews and suggestions, as well as any travel photos. The website is organized by location, so whenever you visit a city you can see all of my posts regarding that location! Happy eating!



Recent Stops

COVID-19 has definitely thrown a wrench into my posting. I get sick easily so I do not feel comfortable enough going out and getting food, so I have been cooking all of my meals at home! My meals are delicious but not the most ~aesthetically~ pleasing so I don’t often take photos of them, but I’m trying to step up my plating game!
