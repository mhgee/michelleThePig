Los Angeles, CA

Everyone knows LA as being the place to be and my foodies heart sure can attest to that! Everywhere you go there is an amazing food option just waiting for you!
The LA area is huge, so it's only natural that each section has their own thing. My favorite part of the LA area was definetly Irvine! I may be biased but this is the area that has a large Japanese population so a lot of chains that I love from Japan are located in the Irvine area. I also tend to enjoy asian food the most, probably beause it's hard to find any where I live and that's the flavor palatte I am more used to, but Irvine really is a great place for food! It's the diversity of LA without as bad traffic.
