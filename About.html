I'm Michelle the Pig!




I am a food lover who is based in Greenville, SC. Being half Japanese, I was raised with the idea of eating food as being a social event. My mother and I would drive up to 5 hours one way just for amazing food that we could not find nearby!

During my time in University I as fortunate enough to be able to spend 6 months studying abroad in Japan. After this time I realized that it was time to start a food blog to document all of my amazing noms!

The goal of this blog is to educate, inform, and hopefully open up the minds of all the readers. I believe that there is no such thing as too much food or even weird food.
