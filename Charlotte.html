Charlotte, NC

Charlotte is a medium sized city that is quickly growing. IT may not be the first place on yopur mind, but if you want some deliciouos local eats, then Charlotte is a great stop. It may not compare to Atlanta when it comes to diversity and choice, but you can still find something worth bragging about.
Since Charlotte is smaller, it's easier to walk around and explore, without feeling overwhelmed. I recommend going to South End or NoDa if you're looking to lazily walk around and grab a bite. If you're in a group where no one has any idea what they want to eat, I recommend Optimist Hall. It's a new food hall that is a step up from a traditional food court found in malls.
