Greenville, SC

Greenville is a smedium (small-medium) sized city in the Upstate of South Carolina. The area is quickly growing due to it being located in the middle of Charlotte and Atlanta, not to mention all of the international businesses who are putting up factories in the area (like BMW).
In Greenville's downtown are you can find an assortment of great restaurants, as well as a beautiful park, Fall's Park, that's worth the stroll. Since Greenville is nestled in the mountains of Upstate South Carolina, there are hundreds of trails to hike, bike, or walk along!
