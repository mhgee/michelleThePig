Atlanta, GA

Atlanta is the capital of Georgia, and it is somewhere I visit frequently! It is a city that is bursting with amazing food, as well as Asian groceries. Throughout college I would frquently make trips to Atlanta just to get boba or do some grocery shopping, even though it was 2 hours away one way!
If you ever in the mood for some authentic korean food, Duluth is your place! Duluth is a suburb of Atlanta and it has a high Korean population that has KBBQ and KFC (Korean Fried Chicken) spots on every corner. If you've ever wanted to eat like your favorite K-Dramas then this is the place to go!
